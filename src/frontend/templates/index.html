Democracy Through Data

Understanding who is registered to vote—and who isn't—is fundamental to a functioning democracy. Our platform aggregates official data from the NC State Board of Elections, making complex electoral information accessible and understandable.

Our Mission To strengthen democracy through transparency and data accessibility, empowering North Carolinians to understand and engage with their electoral system.

Why Voter Data Matters

Transparency

Public access to voter registration data ensures electoral transparency and helps identify potential issues in the registration system.

Engagement

Understanding registration patterns helps organizations and campaigns target outreach efforts to underrepresented communities.

Accountability

Data-driven insights hold election officials accountable and help identify disparities in registration access.

Research

Academic researchers, journalists, and advocates use this data to study electoral trends and inform policy decisions.
